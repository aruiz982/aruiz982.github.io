*_About

I am a Spanish graphics and data reporter, that has been working at the section data of the Spanish paper El Confidencial since 2022.

There, with my colleagues, I make data-based articles and information related to fields such as politics, economics or international affairs, but also demographics or agriculture, which I like a bit better.

Although I write my own articles and make my own graphics, recently maps have taken a big space and time of my work. Maybe it is both because I quite enjoy doing them and exploring new ways of representing geospatial information, but also because my other colleagues are not so fond of such things.

Since this is not my only area of interest, I am currently taking a front-end development bootcamp that will help prepare myself for bigger projects and more complicated scenarios.
